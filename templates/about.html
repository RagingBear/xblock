About Us

About XBlock

XBlock is a data sets sharing platform designed to facilitate the benign development of blockchain and data research. At present, we shares multi-level and multi-angle data of three platforms, including Bitcoin, EOS and Ethereum, as well as relevant research work of InPlusLab in this field. 

Contact Info

If you meet any problems such as dead links, data errors during browsing the platform and using data, please contact us at xblock_blockchain@yeah.net.
We are looking forward to your feedback and suggestions for improving the platform.
